About us

At Betty's Beauty Salon & Spa, not only do we pride ourselves in providing our clients with high quality hairdressing service, we also offer a wide range of beauty treatments- from indulgence treatments such as Facials and Pedicure & Manicure to express beauty maintenance like Lashes, Eyebrow Shape & Tint, plus Hair Removal Waxing.

With 15 years of experience, Betty's beauty service is one you can trust. Our treatments are designed to highlight your natural beauty, health and wellbeing.
